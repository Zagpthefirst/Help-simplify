Procedures for solving:

Forces:

  1. Draw the scenario
  2. If equilibrium, ΣF = 0;If not in equilibrium then ΣF = ma
  3. Make separate FBDs of the relevant objects
  4. Split the forces into components and solve
  5. Check extremes to verify

Tips:

  • Use dot product to find angle between vectors
  • Verify if graph given is trajectory or distance-time graph
  • For projectile at a height, find tf using s = ut + at2/2
  • For finding shortest distance, consider relative motion
  • For collisions, the trajectories should intersect and at the point of intersection the times must be the same
  • Drawing FBD is vital to not making mistakes
  • Dividing forces into x,y,z components can be helpful in solving questions
